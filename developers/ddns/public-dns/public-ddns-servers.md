# Public dWeb dDNS Servers
We maintain 6 public `dWeb dDNS Servers` servers on the `dwebs.io` domain. You are welcomed to use these servers for any of your distributed web projects, all are already pre-configured with any of our native software like [dBrowser](https://dbrowser.io).
